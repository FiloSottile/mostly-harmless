AT Sites

A small prototype that provides listings and Atom feeds for standard.site publications.

These feeds work regardless of what software is used to publish the documents, and keep working even if the author changes handle or moves their data to a different PDS.

Built with Tap by Geomys. On AT Protocol, you can just build things! In very few lines of Go code!

Note that verification is not yet implemented, but may be enforced in the future.
